Full-Day Outings from Chandigarh

Make the most of a free day with scenic drives and hill escapes — Kasauli, Morni Hills, Timber Trail, Pinjore, Shimla and more. Our team can help with cabs, breakfast packs, and late check-ins.

Popular Day Trips (≈35–120 km)

Kasauli ≈55 km

Colonial-era town with pine walks, Christ Church, Gilbert Trail, and golden sunsets. Cafes and calm lanes make it an easy unwind.

Good for: Couples, slow travel, sunset views.

Shimla (Day Trip) ≈115 km

Heritage hill capital with Mall Road, Ridge, and mountain vistas. Start early to enjoy cool air and ample strolling time.

Good for: Family day out, heritage, views.

Location & distances →  •  Rooms & rates →

Morni Hills ≈45 km

Closest hill retreat to Chandigarh — twin lakes, short hikes, and wide-angle valley lookouts. Pleasant even in summers.

Good for: Nature, light hiking, families.

Location & distances →

Timber Trail, Parwanoo ≈35 km

Cable-car glide above the Shivaliks to a ridge-top with photo spots and dining. Quick, scenic, and memorable.

Good for: Scenic ride, short getaway.

Location & distances →

Barog ≈60 km

Quiet hill stop near Kasauli. Pine air, easy trails, and views near the famous Barog tunnel area.

Good for: Picnics, mellow walks, views.

Location & distances →

Chail ≈105 km

Palace lawns, deodars, and the world’s highest cricket ground. Cooler, quieter alternative to Shimla.

Good for: Leisure, photography, forests.

Location & distances →

Kuthar Fort ≈60 km

Historic hillside fort near Sabathu with vantage points and calm trails — pair with Kasauli or Barog.

Good for: Heritage, light trekking.

Location & distances →

Anandpur Sahib & Virasat-e-Khalsa ≈85 km

Sacred Sikh town and stunning museum. Plan for museum hours and riverside views around evening.

Good for: Culture, heritage, photography.

Location & distances →

Naina Devi ≈110 km

Hilltop Shaktipeeth overlooking Govind Sagar. Use ropeway/steps; mornings are calmer for darshan.

Good for: Pilgrimage, views.

Location & distances →

Pinjore Gardens (Yadavindra) ≈22 km

Mughal-style terraces, fountains, and lawns. Combine with Timber Trail or Morni for a fuller day.

Good for: Families, picnics, evenings.

Location & distances →

Siswan Dam & Wildlife Sanctuary ≈20–30 km

Quiet water views, birds, and forest edge drives. Great when you want nature without a long climb.

Good for: Nature, birding, short walks.

Location & distances →

Ropar (Rupnagar) & Sutlej Wetlands ≈45–55 km

Archaeological museum, riverside ghats, and seasonal wetlands. Pleasant winter outing with easy drives.

Good for: Culture + nature, easy day.
