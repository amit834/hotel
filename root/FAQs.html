1) Bookings & Rates

What’s the best way to get the lowest rate at Hotel The Black Gold?

The best rates are always available when you book directly through our official channels — by calling +91-72775-22222, sending us a WhatsApp message, or using our official booking link. Direct bookings often include perks such as complimentary breakfast on select plans and flexible support.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes. If you find a lower publicly available rate for the same room type, dates, and booking conditions, we’ll match it — provided you contact us before confirming your booking.

How are your room rates calculated?

Rates are based on room category, occupancy, seasonality, and availability. Special events in Chandigarh, such as cricket matches or festivals, may impact pricing.

Are taxes included in the room rate?

All rates are exclusive of applicable GST, which is added at the time of booking or check-in.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash payments at the hotel.

Do you accept international credit cards?

Yes, Visa, MasterCard, and other internationally recognized cards are accepted.

Can I pay in cash at check-in?

Yes, we accept cash payments at check-in or check-out.

Do you require a booking deposit?

For certain high-demand periods, group bookings, or long-term stays, we may request a partial advance to confirm your booking.

What’s your refund policy?

Refunds are processed as per our cancellation policy, which varies based on the booking plan. Please confirm the terms before making your reservation.

How do I modify an existing booking?

Simply call our reservations team or send a WhatsApp message to +91-72775-22222. Changes are subject to availability and rate adjustments.

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel via the same channel you booked through. Cancellations made within the free-cancellation window will be refunded in full.

Do you offer corporate rates?

Yes, special rates are available for corporate clients. Please see Corporate Bookings →.

Are there special rates for travel agents?

Yes, we work with travel partners and offer competitive commissions. Contact us for details.

Do you offer long-term stay discounts?

Yes, discounted rates are available for extended stays. See Long-Term Stay → for more information.

Are there seasonal promotions?

We run periodic offers during festive seasons and off-peak periods. Check our website or call us to know the latest deals.

2) Check-in & Check-out

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in is from 12:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM.

Can I request an early check-in?

Yes, subject to room availability and a nominal charge.

Can I request a late check-out?

Yes, depending on availability. Charges may apply for late check-outs beyond 2:00 PM.

Yes, a half-day or full-day rate may apply depending on the time and duration.

What documents do I need at check-in?

All guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID (passport for international guests).

Can I check in without an ID?

No, valid ID is mandatory for all guests.

Can someone else check in on my behalf?

Yes, but their name must be on the booking, and they must present valid ID.

Can I check in after midnight?

Yes, late-night check-ins are accepted if your booking is confirmed in advance.

Is 24-hour check-in available?

We do not offer rolling 24-hour check-in, but our front desk is open 24×7 to assist guests.

Can I store luggage before check-in or after check-out?

Yes, we provide secure luggage storage for early arrivals and late departures.

Is there a secure luggage storage area?

Yes, your belongings will be safely stored at the front desk until you collect them.
