Our Story

We started The Black Gold to make business, medical, and leisure trips to Chandigarh simpler. That means clean rooms, reliable Wi-Fi, attentive housekeeping, and friendly, no-drama service — all minutes from PGI, PEC, and Panjab University.

We’re independent, local, and focused on the details that matter: quick check-in, flexible dining, and practical add-ons like printing/scanning and wheelchair assistance.
