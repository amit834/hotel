Packages & Customization

Basic

Balloons + simple banner — ideal for quick, tasteful surprises.

  • Color of your choice (subject to availability)
  • Message banner (e.g., Happy Birthday)
  • Discreet setup while you’re away

Premium

Balloons + fresh flowers + themed table décor for cake, wine, or gifts.

  • Curated palette (gold & white, red & gold, or pastels)
  • Rose petals (bed/entrance) with LED accents
  • Personalised message card

Luxury

Full-theme décor with floral arrangements, LED candles, and custom message board.

  • Statement backdrop (space permitting)
  • Expanded floral styling
  • White-glove, surprise reveal
Custom Add-ons

Cakes, champagne/wine, fresh bouquets, and gift arrangements can be organised on request. Transparent quotes provided before confirmation.

Booking Process

1

3

4
